Project Description

The MCCH Token application revolutionizes appointment booking at Thrissur Medical College Chest Hospital's cancer center, enabling users to schedule consultations for the next day based on unit numbers assigned by the hospital. With a capacity of up to 300 bookings daily, this user-friendly platform empowers remote patients and caregivers to conveniently reserve or cancel appointments, eliminating the need for on-site registration. By facilitating efficient appointment management, MCCH Token not only enhances patient satisfaction and caregiver convenience but also contributes to the seamless operation of outpatient services and chemotherapy treatments at the hospital.

By leveraging the MCCH Token system, patients and caregivers experience reduced waiting times and streamlined registration processes, improving overall satisfaction and operational efficiency. This innovative application serves as a catalyst for enhancing healthcare accessibility and patient experience, marking a significant advancement in medical appointment booking technology.
